Welcome to Mijin's homepage!

I am Mijin Yoon, a researcher investigating the mysteries of the Universe. I work on cosmological studies mainly focused on large-scale structures in the Universe by analyzing survey observation data (e.g. Euclid, KiDS, LSST, DLS, WISE, 2MASS). I have expertise in weak lensing, a great tool to probe dark matter distribution using the shape distortion of background galaxies. Feel free to contact me for collaboration and talk opportunities in your institutions.
